Becoming a parent?

Finding parenting a challenge?

We understand what that's like.

Parenting can feel overwhelming and stressful at times. Children can respond in ways which challenge us — sometimes we don’t know what to do.

We can help with all of these challenges.

Families in Mind Psychology is a clinical and therapeutic service specialising in working with families with young children and babies, parents, and those expecting a child.

Research shows it’s worth getting in early. Earlier treatment is easier, prevents future problems and pays dividends for your child’s overall development, and for the whole family.
